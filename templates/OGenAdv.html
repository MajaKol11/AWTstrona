General Advice

This will be a page dedicated for new pet owners offering them essential guidance on how to care for their pets. This page aims to provide advice on key areas of pet care to help new owners feel confident and informed about meeting their pet’s needs. Some of the information in this page will include:

● Energy Levels and Ideal Walk Durations for Each Breed
● Ideal Weight for Each Breed
● How and When to Feed
● Pet Behaviour and Body Language
● Training Tips
● Health and Grooming Recommendations

Read on to find out more about these essential topics and ensure you are fully equipped to meet your pet's physical, emotional, and behavioral needs.
